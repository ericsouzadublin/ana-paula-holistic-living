About Ana Paula

A journey from architecture to a more complete way of supporting life, space, and wellbeing.

A personal and professional path


With over 10 years of experience in architecture, Ana Paula has worked closely with spaces and people’s dreams, developing a refined and grounded approach to design.

Over time, her path expanded beyond the physical space — through personal experiences, therapy, and a deeper connection with natural and alternative practices.
